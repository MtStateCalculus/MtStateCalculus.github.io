Thus we can see that the left Riemann sum is given by
\begin{equation*} \int_{1}^{2}\,e^{x}\,dx \approx e^{1}\cdot 0.5 + e^{1.5}\cdot 0.5 \end{equation*}
and the right Riemann sum by
\begin{equation*} \int_{1}^{2}\,e^{x}\,dx \approx e^{1.5}\cdot 0.5 + e^{2}\cdot 0.5. \end{equation*}
From the sketches we can see that the right Riemann sum is an overestimate and the left Riemann sum an underestimate.
