Since the function \(f(x)=16 - x^2\) is even,

\begin{equation*} \int_{-4}^{4} (16 -x^2)\, dx = 2 \int_{0}^{4} (16 -x^2)\, dx = 2 \left. \left(16 x - \frac{x^3}{3}\right) \right|_ {0}^{4} = 2 \left[ \left(16 \cdot 4- \frac{4^3}{3}\right) - (0-0) \right] = 2 \frac{128}{3} = 85.3333333333333\text{.} \end{equation*}
