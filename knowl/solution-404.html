Solution 6.5.5.3.1.

\(w = 1 + e^{5 x}\text{,}\) we have
\begin{equation*} \int_{-\infty}^{0}\,{e^{5 x}\over 1 + e^{5 x}}\,dx = \lim\limits_{b\to 1^{+}} \int_b^{1 + e^{0}} {1\over 5}{dw\over w} = \lim\limits_{b\to 1^{+}} {1\over 5}(\ln(1 + e^{0}) - \ln(b)) = {1\over 5}\ln(1 + e^{0}). \end{equation*}
