Exercise 1. Limits of five sequences.

Match the formulas with the descriptions of the behavior of the sequence as \(n\to\infty\text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle s_n = n\sin(n)/(n+1)\)

  2. \(\displaystyle s_n = \cos(1/n)\)

  3. \(\displaystyle s_n = 1 - n^2\)

  4. \(\displaystyle s_n = 3 + (-1)^n/(n)\)

  5. \(\displaystyle s_n = (n+1)/n\)

  1. converges to one from below

  2. converges to one from above

  3. does not converge, but doesn't go to \(\pm\infty\)

  4. converges to three from above and below

  5. diverges to \(-\infty\)
