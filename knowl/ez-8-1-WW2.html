Exercise 1. Limits of five sequences.

Match the formulas with the descriptions of the behavior of the sequence as \(n\to\infty\text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle s_n = n\sin(n)/(n+1)\)

  2. \(\displaystyle s_n = n(n+1) - 1\)

  3. \(\displaystyle s_n = 1/(n+1)\)

  4. \(\displaystyle s_n = 1 + \cos(n)/n\)

  5. \(\displaystyle s_n = \cos(1/n)\)

  1. diverges to \(\infty\)

  2. converges to one from below

  3. converges to one from above and below

  4. does not converge, but doesn't go to \(\pm\infty\)

  5. converges to zero through positive numbers
