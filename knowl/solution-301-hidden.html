\(F'(x)=e^{-x^2/5}\) and \(F(0)=2\text{,}\) we have
\begin{equation*} F(x)=F(0)+\int_0^x e^{-t^2/5}\,dt = 2 + \int_0^x e^{-t^2/5}\,dt. \end{equation*}
Substituting \(x=2\) and evaluating the integral numerically gives
\begin{equation*} F(2)=2 + \int_0^{2} e^{-t^2/5}\,dt \approx 3.57363. \end{equation*}
