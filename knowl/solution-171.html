Solution 2.7.3.1.1.

We differentiate implicitly with respect to \(x\text{.}\) This gives

\begin{equation*} 5 x^{4} y - x^{5} \, \frac{dy}{dx} - 1 - 8 \, \frac{dy}{dx} = 0, \end{equation*}

or

\begin{equation*} (x^{5}-8) \, \frac{dy}{dx} = 1-5x^{4}y, \end{equation*}

and so

\begin{equation*} \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1 - 5 x^{4} y}{x^{5} - 8}. \end{equation*}
